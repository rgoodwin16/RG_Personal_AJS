Ray Goodwin

Web Developer & Web Developer

I love making things. Whether it is great art, elegant code or using both to tell a new story I just really enjoy creating something from nothing.

For most of my career, I developed creative and innovative solutions to meet the daily challenges of managing a concrete production facility and over a dozen employees. Throughout the course of my 14 year career I have been known for my leadership and creative problem solving skills. I have over 10 years of management experience and am a self-starter who is constantly learning new and better ways of creating high quality content.

As an avid gamer and comic book fan since age 5 technology, art and great story telling have been a huge part of my life. While working as the plant manager for a concrete company, I rekindled my love of programming and coding and embarked on a journey to teach myself how to build websites with HTML and CSS and to manage them using PHP by teaching myself WordPress. I am also self-taught and have several years of experience with Adobe Photoshop and Dreamweaver and over two years of experience using Daz 3D Studio.

You can see from my portfolio I have created and published my own digital comic book and have developed, launched and maintain a website for it. All of the content on IWOAComics.com and Callahan are the result of that creative journey which have led me to turn my creative passion, my desire for learning and my problem solving skills into my career.

I am a web designer, developer and 3D artist with expertise in HTML5, CSS, JavaScript, and jQuery, JSON, MVC, PHP, WordPress, Photoshop, Visual Studio and Daz 3D Studio. I am meticulous, creative and I love to collaborate with other likeminded individuals to create beautiful content.

Creating is not just a job for me, it's my passion.
